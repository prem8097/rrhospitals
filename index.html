Ever since the inception of Ahalia Group in 1984 in the Emirate of Abu Dhabias a small clinic, the focus to provide high-quality medical services to its patients continued even when the hospital was built on a small scale with minimum resources. The vision of group founder was simple and straightforward; to bring international standards of health care within the reach of common man in the UAE as envisaged by him. It was possible only by the trust that people bestowed upon us and today Ahalia Group has grown into a conglomerate that is serving humankind across the UAE, with Two multi-specialty hospitals, 20 satellite medical centers in the peripheral areas of UAE along with 35 Pharmacies providing high quality services and products to its patients. The group also operates a line of Ophthalmology Centers and Optical shops sprouting under the brand name Unique Ahalia Ophthalmology Medical Centers in UAE. Ahalia Group has always kept high facility medical services as its foremost priority. With relentless hard work and efforts of a team of devoted people Ahalia Group has prospered greatly; not overnight but in the time where a team of selfless people worked day and night to achieve one mutual goal- The goal of serving human kind. What makes AHALIA distinctive is indeed our dedicated team of doctors and paramedical staff, who are always committed to improvement and delivery of high quality care. Due to exemplary medical care, our hospital’s services have been acknowledged and it is ranked as one of the top-notch hospitals in UAE. The pharmaceutical branch of AHALIA provides the day-to-day requirements in Medicare through our retail pharmacy chain over 35 localities across the different emirates. Since our inception in 1984, we have grown in both size and scope of services offered. Other than the multi specialty Hospitals in Abu Dhabi, we have 20 satellites Medical Centres in the peripheral areas of Mussafha, Baniyas, Al Ain, MadinaZayed, Mirfa, Gayathi, Dubai, Sharjha, Ajman, Ras al Khaima and Fujairah. This expansion was done with the aim to provide comprehensive and budgeted medical care to the rural population in remote areas through the network of clinics in these areas. All of our branches have highly qualified Doctors and support staffs to help you confront your health care challenges. All the centres are empanelled with all major insurance companies and attached with Pharmacies. The hospital is supported by 24X7 emergency services working 365 days a year equipped with latest life-saving equipment matching international standards, with fully-fledged ambulance services. The quality of medical facilities is not just claimed upon verbally- Our services have been recognized officially by ISO9001, JCI, and CAP Accreditations and we are the proud recipients of ‘Sheikh Khalifa Excellence Model’ appreciation award. AHALIA has also diversified to other services which includes Exchange houses and Educational institutions in UAE. Ahalia Group started with a dream and is now serving with high grade medical facilities across UAE. Our goal, however, does not end here. We wish to continue our mission with the same spirit and take it farther to provide the best of our medical services on a wider level.
